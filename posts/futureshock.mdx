---
title: "Future Shock: The Inevitability of Change"
date: 09/01/2023
---

One of the challenges we face as we merge deeper with technology is the phenomenon of "future shock." Rapid technological and societal changes can lead to disorientation and distress. But as I've come to realize, change is not an end, but a continuation. In the dance between progress and preservation, the key is enlightenment. Knowledge and understanding are the torchbearers that can guide us through the murkiness of change.
