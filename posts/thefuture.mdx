As we stand at the crossroads of technology and humanity, the journey ahead is uncertain. But with awareness, understanding, and a reimagined approach to education, we can navigate the future with hope and purpose. After all, in the words of Toki Pona, "tenpo kama li toki e sona sin." - The future brings new knowledge.
